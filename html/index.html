Hidden gems

Low Lantern

Low Lantern was a converted three-mast ship, the Low Lantern, used as a festhall, tavern, and gambling house in Baldur's Gate. It was often used as a place for covert meetings.

Basilisk Gate

The Basilisk Gate was a gate and local landmark in Baldur's Gate, situated in the outer city wall, that separated the Lower City district of Eastway from the Outer City district of Stonyeyes.

Bonecloak's Apothecary

The apothecary is a flowering ivy-covered building with two floors and attic space. A small greenhouse was built into the shop's side.
